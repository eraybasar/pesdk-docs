EXPOSURE_MODE

EXPOSURE_MODE

Value is int. This tag indicates the exposure mode set when the image was shot. In auto-bracketing mode, the camera shoots a series of frames of the same scene at different exposure settings.

  • 0 = Auto exposure
  • 1 = Manual exposure
  • 2 = Auto bracket
  • Other = reserved
