MAX_APERTURE_VALUE

MAX_APERTURE_VALUE

Value is unsigned double. Maximum aperture value of lens. You can convert to F-number by calculating power of root 2 (same process of ApertureValue(0x9202). 


          FNumber = Math.exp( MaxApertureValue * Math.log( 2 ) * 0.5 )
