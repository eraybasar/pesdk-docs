SHUTTER_SPEED_VALUE

SHUTTER_SPEED_VALUE

Value is signed double. Shutter speed. To convert this value to ordinary 'Shutter Speed'; calculate this value's power of 2, then reciprocal. For example, if value is '4', shutter speed is 1/(2^4)=1/16 second.
