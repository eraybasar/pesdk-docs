METERING_MODE

METERING_MODE

Value is unsigned int. Exposure metering method:

  • 0 = unknown
  • 1 = Average
  • 2 = CenterWeightedAverage
  • 3 = Spot
  • 4 = MultiSpot
  • 5 = Pattern
  • 6 = Partial
  • Other = reserved
  • 255 = other
